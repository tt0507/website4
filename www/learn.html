Cornell Coffee Club Creations

Grind beans at a fineness between 7 and 7 ½. Keep in mind the visual descriptors the recipe provides (coarse sea salt, bread crumbs, etc.) Adjust grind coarser or finer depending on how the extraction goes/tastes.
